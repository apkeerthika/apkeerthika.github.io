TITLE: BALANCED EQUI DISTANCE CLUSTER BASED PARTICLE SWARM OPTIMIZATION IN WIRELESS SENSOR NETWORKS

Wireless Sensor Networks (WSNs) can be defined as a self-configured networks to monitor physical or environmental conditions and pass their data through the network. Energy is one of the most important resources in such networks. The Enhanced Optimized Energy Efficient Routing protocol (E-OEERP) eliminates such individual node formation and improves the overall network lifetime. The E-OEERP shows better performance in terms of energy efficiency is much reduced, throughput, transmission delay is reduced and network life time is prolonged of the nodes.
